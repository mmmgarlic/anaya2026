PROJECT TITLE:

Thirsty Robots

CREATIVE CODE GAME DESIGN WEB DESIGN & AI CHATBOT CREATION

Thirsty Robots is a series of playful interventions that explore our growing reliance on Artificial Intelligence and the hidden environmental cost behind it — specifically, AI’s water usage.

Click here to see it for yourself!

Thirsty Robots comprises a series of three interactive websites, each offering a distinct visual and conceptual approach, accompanied by a fourth, archival site presenting them all-together:

The Original Thirsty Robot

Simple chatbot interface – inspired by ChatGPT – but with a water bottle tracker.

The Island Bot

More immersive interface, drawn in Procreate and animated with JavaScript.

Thirsty Robot: The Game!

A water-draining game – click as quick as you can! Accompanied by facts about AI’s environmental impact.
